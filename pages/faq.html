Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download music?

Once you purchase a track or album, it will appear in your Library where you can download it anytime.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We support mobile money, credit/debit cards, and select local African payment options.

How do I reset my password?

Visit the “Forgot Password” page and follow the instructions to reset your account credentials.

Can artists upload their own music?

Yes! Artists can sign up for a JENGU Artist account and upload albums or tracks from their dashboard.
